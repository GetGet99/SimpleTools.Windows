Simple Tools - Base 64 Converter

Extra Info: Encoding and decoding base64 without opening DevTools.

Easily convert base64 encoded string to and from text. This is a UI wrapper of atob and btoa JavaScript functions. No longer needed to open devtools to decode them!

Simple Tools are tools run locally in your browser. Easily accessible from all your devices with internet connections. Whatever data you input here stays within your device. No ads and data are being sent out to the internet.
